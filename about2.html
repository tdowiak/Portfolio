About Me

A self-taught web developer in Pittsburgh, PA, I earned my B.S. in Materials Science and Engineering from Penn State University. After 2 years of working in heavy manufacturing and steel, I realized it just wasn't for me. I've always enjoyed the challenge of coding and finally decided to commit myself to a career as a developer. I've been spending the last several months teaching myself HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java and look forward to putting my new skills to use in a professional setting.
