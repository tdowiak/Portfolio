About Me

A self-taught web developer in Pittsburgh, PA, I earned my B.S. in Materials Science and Engineering from Penn State University. After 2 years of working in heavy manufacturing and steel, I decided to change career paths. I've always enjoyed the challenge of coding and finally asked myself, "why not pursue this as a career?" I've since spent the last few months teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and look forward to using my new skills in a professional setting as a web developer.
